Colour categories

There are five colour categories available, named category-a (green), category-b (bronze), category-c (silver), category-d (blue) and category-e (yellow).

In addition to this, course pages have their own specialised aliases for degree level undergraduate (green), certificates_diplomas, n_award (bronze), coursework (silver), phd (blue) and research (yellow).

Pre-select filters

Filters can be pre-selected on page entry by adding ?filter=label to the URL, where label is the "colour category" from above. eg. Load this page with category B pre-selected.
